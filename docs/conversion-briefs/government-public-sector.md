## 20. Differentiation Angle vs Competitors
- **vs Big-4 (EY, PwC, Deloitte, KPMG, Accenture):** Brocode delivers production code on the entity's own infrastructure; the Big-4 model sub-contracts delivery to an offshore SI, which the tender pack discloses by name. The "advice vs delivery" framing is explicit.

- **vs in-house build:** five-week working pilot vs a typical 12–18 month in-house AI programme; the Arabic model stack is pre-trained.
