## 10. The Unique Mechanism
- **Arabic-aware product discovery:** a fine-tuned **AraBERT-v2** + multilingual **mE5** embeddings powering vector search over the product catalogue, deployed alongside **Algolia / Coveo** or as a replacement; handles Khaleeji dialect queries, abbreviations, and Arabic-English code-switching.
- **Demand forecasting:** a hierarchical model combining **temporal fusion transformers** with a calendar-aware feature store keyed to Ramadan/Eid/Hajj cycles, payday cycles (UAE 25th, KSA 1st), school terms by emirate, weather, and tourism arrivals (DET/DCT data).
- **Dynamic pricing:** Bayesian price-elasticity models per SKU-store-day, with guardrails for MRRP, MAP, regulator-sensitive categories (pharma, baby formula in KSA, ADAFSA-monitored items).
- **RFM and customer intelligence:** a CDP-agnostic feature store on **Snowflake / Databricks / BigQuery** with a churn-and-CLV model that handles the long retail tail.
- **Store ops:** CCTV-based queue and shelf-availability detection on **NVIDIA Jetson** edge devices, with a privacy-preserving inference pattern that does not store face data.
- **Commerce stack integrations:** named connectors for **Salesforce Commerce Cloud, SAP Commerce, Adobe Commerce / Magento, Oracle Retail, VTEX, Shopify Plus**.
