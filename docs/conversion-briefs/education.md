## 8. The Stakes
- An ADEK or KHDA inspection that flags weak personalisation evidence becomes a public school-rating outcome and a parent-acquisition impact within one term.

- A student-attrition spike that the early-warning model did not catch costs a university USD 25–60 K per lost student over the cycle.
- A generative-AI policy void becomes a board-level governance question and a staff-trust issue with the teaching faculty.
