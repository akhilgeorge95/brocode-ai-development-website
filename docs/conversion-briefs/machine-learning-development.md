## 9. The Promise (H1 Direction)
Production ML that ships in 12 weeks, monitored, retrainable, and auditable against CBUAE / DFSA model-risk standards — built by a named pod of senior engineers on the ground in Dubai and Abu Dhabi, not a slide-ware practice or an offshore contractor.

## 10. The Unique Mechanism
- A fixed-shape **ML Delivery Pod**: one ML lead, two senior MLEs, one MLOps engineer, one data engineer, one solution architect — all UAE-resident or relocatable, all on a single engagement contract.
- The **Brocode Production-Ready Reference Architecture**: Feature store (**Feast**), experiment tracking (**MLflow**), orchestration (**Airflow** or **Prefect**), serving (**BentoML** or **NVIDIA Triton**), monitoring (**Evidently AI** + **Prometheus**), drift alerting wired into the client's existing Splunk or Datadog.
- The **12-Week Production Path**: discovery (2 weeks), prototype on real data (4 weeks), production hardening with paired retraining (4 weeks), regulator-ready handover (2 weeks). Fixed-price option available.
- A documented **Model Risk Pack** template aligned to CBUAE Model Risk Management Standards (effective 2024) and DFSA SEMC guidelines — covers data lineage, validation evidence, challenger models, and ongoing monitoring plan.
- Partner ecosystem: **NVIDIA Inception**, **AWS Advanced Tier**, **Databricks**, **G42 Cloud**, **Snowflake** — same engineers hold certifications across all four.
