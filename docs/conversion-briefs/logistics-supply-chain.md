- **Demand forecasting:** hierarchical models keyed to GCC calendar effects (Ramadan, Eid, Hajj, DSF/DSS, school terms by emirate, payday cycles), with cross-border lane awareness for UAE–KSA, UAE–Oman, UAE–Qatar and the new GCC Customs Union flows.
- **Customs risk scoring:** entity-and-shipment risk models integrating **Mirsal 2** (Dubai Customs), **FASAH/Fasah** (KSA), **Bayan** (KSA Customs), and the **WCO SAFE** framework; AEO-aware (Authorised Economic Operator) treatment; reduces red-channel false positives while preserving SAR-equivalent compliance signals.
- **Port and yard optimisation:** integration with **Navis N4** and **CATOS** via the **TOS Adapter** layer; berth-and-yard sequence models, equipment-pool deployment, and a documented latency target for live decisions.
- **TMS / WMS integration:** named connectors for **Blue Yonder TMS & WMS, SAP TM, SAP EWM, Manhattan Active, Oracle TMS, Körber WMS**.
- **Cold-chain and high-value cargo:** IoT-fused exception models on **Roambee / Tive / Sensitech** telemetry, with regulator-aware temperature deviation reporting (pharma, fresh, dairy).
- **GCC trade-corridor data layer:** unified feature store covering the UAE, KSA, Oman, Qatar, Bahrain and Kuwait trade calendars.
