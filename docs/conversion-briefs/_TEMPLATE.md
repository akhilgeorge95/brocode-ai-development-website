# Conversion Brief Template

> This is the schema every page brief in `/docs/conversion-briefs/` must follow.
> Each of the 20 fields must be filled out **specifically and uniquely** for the page.
> No "TBD". No copy-paste between pages. If two briefs feel interchangeable, one is wrong.

---

## 1. Page Slug & URL
`/<section>/<page-slug>`

## 2. Primary Audience Persona
Role, industry, company size, country, mandate. Be specific enough that a copywriter knows who they are writing to. Avoid generic "decision-maker".

## 3. Visitor Intent at Arrival
What they just searched, what they're hoping to find on this page, what stage of the buying cycle (awareness / evaluation / decision).

## 4. Primary Search Keyword + Ad Group
The single Google Ads keyword this page is built to convert (exact / phrase / broad match noted). Pair with the ad group name.

## 5. Secondary Keyword Cluster
5–8 related long-tail keywords this page also targets organically.

## 6. Geographic Targeting
Primary cities/emirates, secondary GCC countries, tertiary international markets.

## 7. The One Painful Problem
The specific business pain this visitor is feeling right now, in one sentence. Concrete, not abstract.

## 8. The Stakes
What happens to this visitor's KPI / career / business if they don't solve it. Specific consequences.

## 9. The Promise (H1 Direction)
The specific, quantified outcome this page promises. Direction for the H1, not the H1 itself.

## 10. The Unique Mechanism
The specific Brocode capability, partnership, or methodology that makes this promise believable. Concrete — name tools, frameworks, partners, methodologies.

## 11. Top 3 Objections
The exact objections this specific audience will raise. List as O1, O2, O3.

## 12. Proof Strategy
What specific proof closes those objections. Reference clients (anonymised), benchmarks, certifications, partner badges, case-study angles.

## 13. The Lead Magnet
A service-specific downloadable that earns the email. Specific title, format, length, key figure.

## 14. Primary CTA Wording
Exact button text tuned to this page.

## 15. WhatsApp Pre-filled Message
Exact message text that appears when the WhatsApp button is tapped.

## 16. Quote Form Fields
The 4–6 specific qualification fields this page's form asks for, beyond name/email/org.

## 17. Hero Media Direction
Exact visual concept for the hero — what's on screen, the camera move, the colour/light treatment, any overlaid UI or data.

## 18. Section-by-Section Outline
The unique section order and angle for this specific page. Numbered list — 6–10 sections, each with a one-line angle. NOT a generic template.

## 19. Internal Linking Strategy
The 5 specific other pages this page should link to and the reason for each link.

## 20. Differentiation Angle vs Competitors
How this page positions against the specific competitors a visitor is likely also evaluating. Name them.

## Production notes (optional)
- Anchor visuals or screenshots to source
- Any compliance constraints
- Any seasonal / event-driven timing
